Support and Information for Black Women Facing Pregnancy Challenges

Welcome Message

Thank you for taking the step to seek support. You are not alone, and help is available. Below, you'll find resources tailored to meet your needs—whether you're looking for emotional, physical, or spiritual guidance. We are here to help you!

Advice & Aid Pregnancy Resource Program

Who We Are:

Advice & Aid is here to walk alongside you during your pregnancy journey and beyond. We provide confidential, compassionate, and judgment-free support.

Our Services Include:

  • Free pregnancy tests and ultrasounds.
  • Abortion pill reversal (time-sensitive assistance available).
  • Counseling and emotional support.
  • Parenting classes and life skills workshops.
  • Material support (e.g., diapers, clothing, baby gear).
  • Referrals to medical care, housing, and other community resources.
